Landry Allbright is an actress. She debuted as Bridget Forrester in the soap opera The Bold and the Beautiful in 1996. Afterward, she had her first film role in Con Air. In the 2000s, she mostly had guest roles in several shows, including Malcolm in the Middle, The Invisible Man, Spin City, Judging Amy, 7th Heaven, and Will & Grace, among others. At the same time, she also had performances in such films as How the Grinch Stole Christmas and Beautiful. Later in the 2010s, she had roles in the films Last Hours in Suburbia and Feral, as well as guest-starred in Happy Thoughts, Love Daily, and S.W.A.T. Her most recent role was in the 2020 film Getaway.
