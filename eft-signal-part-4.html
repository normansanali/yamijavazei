Signal – Part 4 is a quest in Escape From Tarkov. The quest requires you to reach Memory skill level 4. It becomes available after Signal – Part 3.

Escape From Tarkov Signal – Part 4 Quest Details

Signal – Part 4 Quest Dialogue

I’ve been thinking about that thing we discussed last time. Have people really become kinder with the development of civilization? Do they really go hand in hand? All this time, where we believe there is civilization, life has become more comfortable, easier. There were increasingly fewer worries about safety or getting something to eat. It’s easy to be humane when nothing is threatening you. The answer to these questions was right before my eyes. The situation in Tarkov has shown us how much kinder we have become. Once the issues of survival came to the front, it turned out that decent people aren’t more common than maybe 500 years ago. There’re probably even fewer of them now. What do you think, are you one of them? Okay, don’t overthink it. I need you to do something form me. However, you will need to remember quite a lot for this job. Practice memorizing large volumes of information. I’ll contact you later.

Objectives

  • Reach the required Memory skill level 4

Rewards

  • +8600 XP
  • ~20,000 Roubles
  • +0.02 reputation with Mechanic
  • 1x Nightforce ATACR 7-35×56 riflescope

Reward Dialogue

Seventh digit after the decimal separator in the pi number? IIO? Okay, when the time comes, I’ll contact you.

Guide

By the time you reach this quest, you should have played enough raids that would boost your memory skill to 4. Basically, the more you play the more you boost your Memory skill. The memory skill in Escape From Tarkov serves for the following:

  • Trained Skills last longer before they start to fade
    • Decreases the rate at which you forget skills
    • Decreases rollback of skill levels
