AJ Styles: Fatal Four-Way At WWE Royal Rumble Has Recipe For Unbelievable Match

 Posted on July 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  358 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

AJ Styles previews his high-profile title match at WWE Royal Rumble.

At WWE Royal Rumble, AJ Styles, LA Knight, and Randy Orton will challenge Roman Reigns for the Undisputed WWE Universal Championship.

In an interview with Josh Martinez, Styles was asked about the differences and similarities between himself, Randy Orton, LA Knight, and Roman Reigns.

“Well, I think we all have in common the fact that we want the WWE Championship,” AJ Styles said. “Randy wants it for his legacy, I want it because it’s been a minute since I’ve had it, LA Knight has never had it, [and] Roman Reigns is just trying to keep it a little bit longer, there’s similarities there. But as far as us…we are all completely different wrestlers, there’s no doubt about that.

“Very different in so many different ways, you got the RKO, the three deadliest letters in wrestling. And LA Knight, he’s not new to wrestling, but he’s the newest guy in this match to WWE, and he’s made an impact really quick. And then of course, you got Roman Reigns, whose…gosh, how long has he had the championship? Three years?”

AJ Styles: We Have A Recipe For An Unbelievable Match

AJ Styles went on to highlight the differences between the competitors. He stated that they will give the fans everything they want, and they have a recipe for an unbelievable match.

“Listen, we’re all different in so many different ways,” AJ Styles said. “Because of size comparison, ability in the ring, we’re all different. So I think that’s a recipe for a great wrestling match, you know? When you got so many different things going on, and it’s going to come together, and we’re going to give you, the fans, everything you want in entertainment and wrestling and whatnot.

“It’s a match that can go anywhere, anything can happen, anything can be used. So, again, it’s a recipe for an unbelievable match and I think we’re going to pull it off.”
