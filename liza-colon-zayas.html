Is Liza Colón-Zayas Married? Relationship

The actress from ‘Law & Order: Special Victims Unit’ is married to David Zayas, a fellow Dexter actor and co-star. The two had a few dates before exchanging vows on November 21, 1998.

From their union, they had two children: a boy and a girl named Emma Zayas. The four-person family is enjoying themselves immensely.

How did Liza Colón-Zayas start her Professional Career?

After landing a role in an Off-Broadway show, Liza launched her career. She subsequently made her breakthrough into popular culture when she acted in, developed, and produced the one-woman show Sistah Supreme.

Since then, the actress from the Bronx has made appearances in TV programs including Hope & Faith, Third Watch, Sex and the City, and New York Undercover.

In 2010, she had a significant role in the drama Dexter on Showtime. From 2004 until 2015, she played Cyndi, Delores Rodriguez, and Dolores Rodriguez in the law enforcement drama Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.

She joined the Titans cast in 2018 as Detective Jessica Perez. A year later, she was chosen to play Principal Fallow in the David Makes Man television series.

Her most recent appearance was in the big-budget movie The Woman in the Window from 2021. Together with Uzo Aduba, Dianne Wiest, and Michelle Forbes, she is now starring in the drama In Treatment as Rita / Rita Ortiz.
