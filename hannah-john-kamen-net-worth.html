Hannah John-Kamen is a British actress. In 2011, she debuted in an episode of Misfits, followed by another one-time role in Black Mirror. She later appeared as Roxy in 2 episodes of Whitechapel. She then played Rosa Maria Ramírez in 4 episodes of the BBC series The Hour. In 2015, she made her big screen debut with a small role in the movie Star Wars: The Force Awakens. That same year, she started starring as Dutch / Aneela Kin Rit in the Canadian series Killjoys. This year, she gained wider recognition for playing Sophie in Tomb Raider, F’Nale Zandor in Ready Player One, and Ava Starr / Ghost in Ant-Man and the Wasp.
