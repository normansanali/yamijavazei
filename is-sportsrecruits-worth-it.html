SportsRecruits: A Closer Look

SportsRecruits is a college recruiting website that connects student-athletes with college coaches. Founded in 2008, SportsRecruits boasts partnerships with over 2,000 colleges and universities across the United States. The website offers a range of services, including the creation of a recruiting profile, communication with college coaches, and access to recruiting events and camps. SportsRecruits also offers a mobile app, allowing student-athletes to stay connected on the go.

One of the key features of SportsRecruits is its ability to track college coach engagement. The website notifies student-athletes when a coach views their profile, watches their highlight video, or sends them a message. SportsRecruits also provides analytics on profile views, allowing student-athletes to see which colleges are showing interest in them.

However, SportsRecruits does come with a cost. The website offers a range of subscription plans, with prices starting at $49 per month. While the website does offer a free trial, the cost of a subscription can add up over time, especially for student-athletes who are on a tight budget.
