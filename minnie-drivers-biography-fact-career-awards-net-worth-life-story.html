Finsbury Park of London is where Minnie Driver was born. She started her stage career in 1991. Her mother was Gaynor Churchward, a designer as well as former model, and the father was Ronnie Driver, a businessman. Interestingly, Minnie’s mom was the mistress of her father, and her father’s wife did not know of the existence of this family. Driver went to the Bedales and later on to the Webber Douglas Academy of Dramatic Art. She has Italian, Welsh, Scottish, French and Irish ancestry.

Minnie Driver has received the nomination for the Oscars for the Best Supporting Actress for her role in the film Good Will Hunting. She was also nominated for a Golden Globe and the Emmy award, and for her work in the TV series The Riches. She belonged to the band “Puff, Rocks and Brown”. They had a deal with Island Records. Minnie returned to performing music in 2000. In 2001, she signed a deal with EMI and Rounder Records.

On the 5th of September, 2008, Driver had a boy – Henry Story Driver. It was not revealed who the father of her child was. In 2012 February, Minnie said that her son’s father was the writer of her show The Riches. She was engaged once with Josh Brolin, but later they split on amicable terms.
