Season 6 of Ready To Love will begin on OWN tv on July 29 with 20 new singletons. The upcoming season will be hosted by Tommy or Thomas Miles, who will counsel the candidates to pursue ‘love and not desire.’ The famous host and well-known comedian have a net worth of about $10 million as of April 2023. He is also a comedian, screenwriter, film producer, and entrepreneur. Will Packer, Kelly Smith of Will Packer Media, Jeff Spangler, and Rob LaPlante of Lighthearted Entertainment executive produce Season 2 of the dating program

Ready To Love’s official summary is as follows:

Will Packer’s famous dating series, presented by Thomas “Nephew Tommy” Miles, delves into the real-life dating encounters of gorgeous, accomplished, and mature Black men and Black women in their 30s and 40s, all of whom are seeking enduring love and a genuine connection.

Thomas Miles from Ready To Love Season 6 has a theater degree.

Thomas “Nephew Tommy” Miles, born on May 18, 1967, graduated from Texas A&M University with a degree in theatre. He also joined the Royal Shakespeare Company while studying for his degree. Thomas was cast in a number of roles via the theatrical company.

At the start of his acting career, he landed a big part in the main cast of David M. Matthews’ crime thriller film N-Secure (2010). Following that, he appeared in The Heart Specialist (2011), a romantic comedy directed, written, and co-produced by Dennis Cooper. He also appeared in another comedy film, Think Like a Man (2012), which was based on Steve Harvey’s book Act Like a Lady, Thinks Like a Man (2009). The picture was a huge hit, grossing $96.1 million.

After dabbling in acting, the Ready To Love presenter moved on to stand-up comedy, where he rose to prominence. He appeared at the Essence Music Festival and opened for Luther Vandross. He also taped an hour-long special event at Detroit’s famed Motor City Casino, which is well-known among stand-up comedians.

Miles is also well-known for his work as a radio host. He was cast with Steve Harvey, Shirley Strawberry, and Carla Ferrell in the radio show The Steve Harvey Morning Show, which debuted in 2000 and continues to this day. Steve Harvey and Rushion McDonald established a well-known radio show. The radio program was so popular that it was adapted for television as Steve Harvey Projects on Centric. Tommy was chosen to host the program and quickly became a fan favorite because of his smart quips and funny prank calls.

Furthermore, his prank calls were so well-known that he issued seven studio albums including them: Nephew Tommy’s Prank Phone Calls: Volumes 1-6, Nephew Tommy’s Celebrity Prank Phone Calls, and Nephew Tommy’s Lost Prank Phone Calls: Parts 1 and 2. Furthermore, while on a tour to military sites in the Middle East with the Chairman of the Joint Chiefs, he gained a strong empathy for the military sector. As a consequence, he established the Miles of Giving charity, which assists former/retired/wounded troops. The inaugural episode of Ready To Love will air on CBS Network on July 29 at 8 p.m. ET.
