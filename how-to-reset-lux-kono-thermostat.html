How do I reset my Lux Kono thermostat?

How do I reset my thermostat?
  • Take the front of the thermostat off the wall and look for a small white round reset button on the right side labeled “H.W. Reset”.
  • Hold this button down for at least 5 seconds.
  • The screen should fully populate temporarily.

    Is there a reset button on a thermostat?

    Resetting a non-programmable digital thermostat simply requires taking the face off and pressing the reset button for five seconds or removing the batteries for five seconds. After doing one or both of those options, your thermostat should reset.

    How do I reset my Lux 500 thermostat?

    If you need to reset your Luxpro thermostat, simply hold down the Reset button for five seconds. The padlock icon will now be in the unlocked position.

    Why is my luxpro thermostat not working?

    One of the most common troubleshooting techniques that can help you out from most of the tight corners is restarting the thermostat. Afterward, you can use the same power button to turn it back on and that will fix most of the issues with your thermostat.

    Why can’t I change the temperature on my thermostat?

    Sometimes your thermostat simply needs to be cleaned. Having dust or lint collect inside the thermostat can block the thermostat’s sensors, heating them up so that the room temperature doesn’t match the thermostat setting.
