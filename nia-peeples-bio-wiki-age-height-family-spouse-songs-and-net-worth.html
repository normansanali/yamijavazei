Nia Peeples Biography

Nia Peeples is an American R&B and dance music singer and actress who was born on 10 December 1961 in Hollywood, Los Angeles, California, United States. She is best known for her roles as Sydney Cooke on Walker, Texas Ranger, Nicole Chapman on the hit TV series Fame; Pam Fields, the mother of Emily Fields, on the drama Pretty Little Liars, as well as Karen Taylor Winters on The Young and the Restless
