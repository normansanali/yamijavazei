Miley Cyrus is an iconic American singer, songwriter, and actor.

Miley Ray Cyrus was born Destiny Hope Cyrus on November 23, 1992, in Franklin, Tennessee, United States. She is the daughter of country music musician Billy Ray Cyrus and Leticia “Tish” Jean Cyrus Finley.

Miley Cyrus rose to fame as the title character of the Disney Channel television series Hannah Montana (2006–2011). She was raised alongside her siblings Noah Cyrus, Christopher Cody, Brandi Cyrus, Trace Cyrus, and Braison Cyrus.

While she was Hannah Montana, Miley Cyrus had two number-one and three top-five soundtracks on the US Billboard 200, including the top-ten hit “He Could Be the One” on the US Billboard Hot 100.

Miley Cyrus has thirteen top-five albums on the US Billboard 200 and holds the record for the most by a female artist in the twenty-first century. She is the recipient of MTV’s Best Artist of 2013 Award and has entries on the Time 100 list in 2008 and 2014.

Is Billy Ray Cyrus Miley Cyrus real dad?

What did Billy Ray Cyrus do to Miley Cyrus?
