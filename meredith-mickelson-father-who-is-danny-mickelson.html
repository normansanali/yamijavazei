Meet Danny Mickelson, Meredith Mickelson’s Father

1 July 1999-born Meredith Mickelson is the daughter of Danny Mickelson. She loves to go around as “daddy’s girl” and her dad also occasionally writes on social media “So proud of my daughter”. He was all smiles as he walked the first red carpet with her in Hollywood back in 2019.

On 2 July 2022, on her birthday, Danny took to his social media a beautiful picture of him holding just a few-year-old Meredith. And next to it, he wrote, “Can’t believe this sweet girl is 23 today, I love you so much and am always here for you”.

Danny also had a son Daniel Mickelson, who was an actor and had appeared in the 2019 film The Killer Clown Meets the Candy Man. He was only 23 when he died of fentanyl and cocaine toxicity on 4 July 2021. News of his death was shared by Meredith, his younger sister, on her IG. Heartbroken, she made the statement that she lost her brother, best friend & the other half of her heart a day before. Later, in his memory Meredith also permanently got the “guardian angel” inked on the inside of each of her wrists in a delicate cursive font.

Often, Danny also has been seen writing about missing his son on social media posts. On 5 October 2021, for instance, he wrote “My heart and soul, Happy Birthday my sweet boy !!!” next to a throwback pictorial memory of them.

Other than his immediate family, friends like Kaia Gerber and Paris Hilton also mourned David’s loss. David also was friends with Delilah Hamlin, Amelia Hamlin, and Brielle Biermann.

David, at the time of his passing, also was in a relationship with Maddie Haley, a student nurse at Auburn University. So naturally, Maddie too penned a heartfelt tribute to him, where among other things she talked about feeling like her heart has been ripped out of her chest.

Before he was gone, Daniel had just launched his own fashion line called Kids Back Home. In January 2021, he talked about being “super pumped” about his line of streetwear “for the kids back home”. Meredith, his sister, has helped model the brand’s first run of outfits. Also, Daniel was waiting to “spread the good vibes on a bigger scale.”

While dealing with the heartbreak, Meredith has been back to work. She appeared in Kygo’s 2022 music video for “Lost Without You” for example. Meredith also has been featured on the covers of magazines like Harper’s Bazaar, L’Officiel, Noir, and Grazia, now and then.
