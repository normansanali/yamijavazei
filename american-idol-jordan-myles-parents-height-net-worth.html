Jordan Myles On American Idol

Jordan Myles was one of the contestants of American Idol who showed up at the audition but left with something more. He met Haley Slaton the person who he is ready to spend the rest of his life with. Along with the golden ticket to Hollywood, he also took home the ticket to a lifetime of happiness.

He and Haley pursued their relationship and now they have created a family of their own. Read more about it below.

Is American Idol Jordan Myles Haley Slaton’s Baby Daddy?

No, Jordan Myles is not the baby daddy of Haley Myles. She had her son from a previous relationship based on the timeline of their relationship and the birth of her child.

Jordan Myles is the husband of American Idol contestant Haley Slaton. According to the report in New York Post, Haley and Jordan met each other during the first round of American Idol October audition in Austin, Texas. When she performed her solo for the panel of A-listers she was five months pregnant.

It wasn’t long before they got married. They shared their wedding vows on Christmas Eve 2021 in Cedar Rapids, Iowa. A month after their marriage, Haley gave birth to her firstborn Jaelyn Amir Myles in January 2022. Her husband has since moved to Iowa from Louisiana to be with her.

Myles also introduced their son in February via an IG post. He wrote, “Hey Instagram, say hello to my handsome son and my beautiful wife.”

In an interview with The Gazette, Haley told, “We just knew. We started right off the bat talking to each other, and it ended up being an everyday thing. I was already pregnant, and he stepped up to be a father, and I couldn’t thank him enough for that.”

She added further, “We’re very in love, we’re very happy that we met, and I feel like another reason why I was on the show was to meet him.” She gushed about her husband, “That was a great experience — to meet someone that I finally could spend the rest of my life with.”

In December 2021, Jordan took to his Facebook to share about his new wife. In one part of the post he wrote, “Haley is the funniest, God-fearing, most wonderful woman I have ever met. Marrying her has been the second easiest decision in my life. (Second to accepting Christ.)”

For many motherhood could mean the end of their dreams or at least a halt for a while. Meanwhile, Haley feels otherwise. She told, “Just being a mom shouldn’t stop you from following your dreams. I just want to do this for my son and give him a really good life. And I want to show him to never stop chasing his dreams in the future. Never give up on your dreams. Mommy went and chased hers.”

But, Haley and Myles weren’t the only couples American Idol served as a cupid. Gabby Barrett and Cade Foehner were season 16 contestants who met on the program in 2018 and married a year later. In January 2021, they had their first child, Baylah May.

Furthermore, Maddie Poppe, the season 16 winner, is dating Caleb Lee Hutchinson, the season 16 runner-up. Runner-up in Season 3 Diana DeGarmo married Ace Young a participant from season 5, in 2013.

After the recent, audition and golden ticket, Myles updated his Instagram, “Proud husband 🥰”.

American Idol Jordan Myles Parents

Full name Jordan Dion Myles, the singer is one of the two children of his parents Chris Henderson and Jonielle T Myles.

Born on 30 September 1981, his mother Jonielle is 40 years old. Whereas, his father Chris was born on 31 July 1982, making him 39 years old. His family appears to be faithful Christians. According to LinkedIn, Chris earned a BS degree in Professional Aviation from Louisiana Tech University and BS in Computer Science from the same.

Starting from August 1997, Chris has been working as Field Services Engineer at The Purple Guys.

Jordan has one sibling named Jayla Oliver. The other family member we know about Jordan is his aunt Pamela Murray-Henderson of Shreveport, Louisiana.

American Idol Jordan Myles Net Worth

American Idol Jordan Myles has a burgeoning net worth of under $200 thousand. He is an aspiring singer who recorded his first single which he released on SoundCloud in May 2021. Then he released another single My Rescue which he released with a video on YouTube.

Jordan Myles has a military background. He had enrolled in Louisiana National Guard and shared the perks of being in the military. Some of which he is grateful for that he pointed out is free college, a monthly check, and getting to play football.

Speaking of playing football, Jordan played in SoFi Hawaii Bowl in Honolulu, Hawaii in December 2018. He has shared some of the key moments of his as a footballer on his Instagram.

Jordan completed his high school education at Benton High School and completed his college at Louisiana Tech University.

Fun Fact: Jordan also competed as a powerlifter for Benton High School in Division II 181-pound weight class and finished as a runner-up. He scored 1,340 points whereas the winner of the event Melvin Young was able to get 1,390.
