Early Life and Career Beginnings

Margot Robbie’s journey to stardom began in Dalby, Queensland, Australia. She moved to Melbourne in her late teens to pursue acting professionally. Her early career was marked by roles in Australian independent films and television series, most notably as Donna Freedman in the soap opera “Neighbours.” This role would lay the groundwork for her international success.

Breakthrough in Hollywood

Robbie’s breakthrough came with her role in Martin Scorsese’s “The Wolf of Wall Street,” where she starred alongside Leonardo DiCaprio. Her performance garnered critical acclaim and catapulted her into the Hollywood A-list. This role significantly boosted her earning potential and set the stage for future high-paying roles.
