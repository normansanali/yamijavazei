2 Chainz Early Life

2 Chainz was born on September 12, 1977, in College Park, Georgia. He went to North Clayton High School, where he played basketball and graduated second in his class. Furthermore, he was arrested when he was 15 for felony cocaine possession because he was dealing with marijuana. He later attended Alabama State University on a scholarship and played basketball for the school from 1995 to 1997.
