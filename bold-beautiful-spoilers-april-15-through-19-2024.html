The Bold and the Beautiful Spoilers April 15

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  639 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

It is on in Soaps.com’s latest Bold & Beautiful spoilers for the week of Monday, April 15, through Friday, April 19. Not only does R.J. finally find out what happened between Zende, Luna and way too many special mints, but Deacon learns new intel about Sheila’s past from someone who wishes the villainess would stay in the past. Needless to say, everyone from Hope to Carl the Janitor has reason to worry that Deacon is losing his marbles. Is he, though? Is he really? To get the scoop on these teasers and more, all you have to do is keep reading…

Bold & Beautiful spoilers for week of April 15:

Bold & Beautiful spoilers for Monday, April 15:

In Monday’s recap, RJ learns Luna had sex with Zende, Steffy confronts Deacon, and Carter is stunned by Zende’s admission.

Pulling a Cher, Steffy demands that Deacon snap out of it and return to the realm of reality. Ha — or the reality that she wishes were true!

After weeks of hemming and hawing on Luna’s part, R.J. finally learns the truth about his girlfriend, her mom’s special mints and Zende. Come to think of it, maybe a special mint would help him absorb the news more easily.

Wow! It’s been a few years since this Bold & Beautiful scene stealer was on screen. Wait till you see him now.

Bold & Beautiful spoilers for Tuesday, April 16:

In Tuesday’s recap, RJ punches Zende, and Hope opens up to Liam about Thomas.

We thought that Hope had made herself pretty clear already. Nevertheless, she reveals the real reason she turned down Thomas’ proposal to Liam. Was there more to it than we knew?

Speaking of Thomas, we know everyone’s expecting him to hook up with Ivy. But he may just have a curve ball in store for Hope — and Brooke! Read all about it here.

R.J. angrily confronts Zende for sleeping with his girlfriend. Maybe now Zende will stop daydreaming about the night he had sex with a young woman who was too out of it to consent?

Bold & Beautiful spoilers for Wednesday, April 17:

In Wednesday’s recap, RJ rages at Zende, and Deacon tells Finn about Sugar.

Now that ding-dong, the witch is dead — or so they think — Liam and Hope agree to let Beth spend more time with Deacon.

How do you mend a broken heart? That’s what Poppy will be asking herself as she attempts to comfort Luna.

Bold & Beautiful spoilers for Thursday, April 18:

In Thursday’s recap, Deacon stops Lauren in her tracks with one word, RJ lets Luna know where he stands, and Steffy and Finn discuss Deacon’s obsession.

Deacon pursues a new lead — could it be Sugar? — in his quest to prove he’s not crazy.

Finn shares his concern about Deacon with Steffy. Mm-hmm, because if there’s one thing she wants to talk about more than Sheila, it’s Deacon’s continued obsession with Sheila. “How long,” we can just imagine her thinking, “until the kids are SORAS’d into problem children?”

Bold & Beautiful spoilers for Friday, April 19:

In Friday’s recap, Lauren rocks Deacon’s world, and Hope checks on Finn.

Young & Restless’ Lauren Fenmore shares details about Sheila’s past with Deacon. Although, theoretically, being the notorious criminal that she is, wouldn’t her history be well-documented? Wouldn’t there be books on her and even a Netflix documentary?

Rather than focus on their own lives, Hope, Liam, Steffy, and Finn worry about Deacon’s mental health. He’ll be fine. Look at all he’s endured over the years in this photo gallery of his life story.
