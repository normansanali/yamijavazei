Tom Macdonald Biography and Wiki

Tom MacDonald is a Canadian rapper and music producer. He became famous after releasing his first track “Helluvit” in 2018. He has a youtube channel where he posted his first video “Wannabe” which he posted in 2014. As of now, the star boasts of more than 500k YouTube Subscribers and over 140,000 Instagram Followers.

He has also produced other tracks such as “Dear Rappers” that has garnered over 400 thousand YouTube views. He released his solo album LeeAnn’s Son in 2014. After this, he released See You Tomorrow, Dream People, and the Whiskey Wars, Deathreats.
