Ford 601 Workmaster – This tractor was one of the models that made the ford 601 Workmaster series. A ford 601 orchard variant was also available, with model number 641-21.

Ford 601 Workmaster is equipped with a Ford EAE Red Tiger gasoline 4-cylinder 8-valve liquid-cooled vertical inline engine, which produces 48.4 hp power at a rated rpm of 2200.

Ford 601 Workmaster is equipped with a Ford diesel 4-cylinder 8-valve liquid-cooled vertical inline engine, which produces 42.7 hp power at a rated rpm of 2200.

Ford 601 Workmaster featured a 4×2 2WD, 3,291 lbs weight, 567 kg Rear lift capacity, and 4 forward and 1 reverse gear.
