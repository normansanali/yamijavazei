What is the Net Worth of Anne Frank? Salary, Earnings

Anne has a net worth of $1.5 million as of 1945. She dealt with the genres of autobiography and biography and spoke German and Dutch. She wrote children’s books, worked as a babysitter, and kept a diary.

With the publication of The Diary of a Young Girl in 1947 (originally published in Dutch as Het Achterhuis; translated into English as The Secret Annex), she gained posthumous fame. In this diary, she describes her life in hiding from 1942 to 1944, while Germany occupied the Netherlands during World War II.

Where was Anne Frank born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Anne, who was born on June 12th, 1929, was 15 years old in 1945. She was raised in a Frankfurt, Germany, upper-middle-class Jewish home.

Otto and Edith Frank were Anne’s parents. Mr. Otto Frank, who was a trader by trade, was Anne Frank’s father.

Mrs. Edith Frank, a Holocaust prisoner, was the name of her mother. She was the only child. Margot Frank is the name of her sister.

She finished her early education at Amsterdam’s 6th Montessori School.

Later, she registered for classes at the Jewish Lyceum. She was incredibly engaged in studies from a very young age and writing in particular.
