Inside Bella Hadid's 22nd Birthday Party

 Posted on July 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  317 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Bella Hadid rang in her 22nd birthday in New York City with a bunch of her famous friends by her side. The supermodel had a small but totally star-studded bash on Tuesday, with guests including sister Gigi Hadid, brother Anwar Hadid, boyfriend The Weeknd, her parents Mohamed and Yolanda Hadid, and BFFs Kendall Jenner, Jaden Smith, and Justine Skye.

Snaps from the party show Bella walking in to the room and seeming a bit shocked as her friends scream, and she hugs her boyfriend tight. It's likely the party was a surprise thrown by her beau and close friends.

Bella rocked a black sequined bustier top, vintage Chanel chain belt from What Goes Around Comes Around, Almaz ripped black trousers, and a neck full of chain chokers for the celebration.

She accessorized with a whole collection of diamond rings on almost every finger and layered cuff bracelets.

Gigi shared photos of her little sister blowing out the candles on her gorgeous four-tier cake. "Celebrating my incredible lil sissy @bellahadid tonight! 22 !!!! Can’t believe. Witnessing you shine and share your loving light with the world fills me with pride, and I become more grateful each year for what a true friend you are to me," she wrote.

The party seemed to have an overall butterfly theme, with the cake covered in red cascading butterflies. The ceilings were decorated with glitter streamers, lanterns—and more butterflies.

The supermodel and her musician boyfriend were not afraid to pack on the PDA during the night's festivities. Friends shared photos of them kissing and hugging throughout the night.

The Weeknd, whose real name is Abel Tesfaye, shared a birthday tribute to Bella earlier in the day with a slideshow of never before seen photos of them, writing, "happy birthday Angel 🖤❤️"

The couple also celebrated with a birthday brunch at Sadelle's restaurant in Soho.
