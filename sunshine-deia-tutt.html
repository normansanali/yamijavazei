Her ex-husband also revealed that the pain medication he began taking following his fourth surgery led to the DUI arrest in the year 2014.

Moreover, Chris Kattan also revealed more details of the injury in his memoir. In the memoir, he claimed that it was the result of the Saturday Night Live sketch that aired on 12 May 2001.

In this episode, her husband threw himself backward on a chair while doing a “Golden Girls” parody. Chris further added that NBC paid for two of his five surgeries to repair the spinal injuries.
