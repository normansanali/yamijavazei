Doria Ragland Reacts to Becoming Grandmother for First Time Now Meghan Markle Is Pregnant

 Posted on August 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  321 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Following an exciting weekend for fans of the royal family, as Princess Eugenie married Jack Brooksbank at Windsor Castle, Kensington Palace announced that Meghan Markle is expecting her first child with Prince Harry.

Meghan's mother, Doria Ragland, has always been extremely close to her daughter, so it's unsurprising that she had the sweetest reaction ever to Meghan's baby news.

According to royal commentator Emily Nash on Twitter, a spokesperson for the palace revealed that Doria "is very happy about this lovely news and is looking forward to welcoming her first grandchild." Basically, Doria is about to be the happiest grandmother of all time:

Just last month, Doria visited Meghan and Harry at Kensington Palace. Importantly, she supported Meghan as the Duchess launched her first solo endeavor as a member of the royal family—a charity cookbook created with a group of women affected by the Grenfell Fire.
