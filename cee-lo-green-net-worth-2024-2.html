Early Life and Career Beginnings

Cee Lo Green, born Thomas DeCarlo Callaway, began his journey in Atlanta, Georgia. His early life was marked by a passion for music, which would eventually lead him to form the Southern hip-hop group Goodie Mob. This was the first step in a career that would span various genres and mediums.
