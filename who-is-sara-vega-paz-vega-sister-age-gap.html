Sara Vega Is A Dancer, Actress, And Singer

Paz Vega’s sister is a flamenco dancer. Not only that, but Sara Vega is an Actress and singer.

Sara’s acting credits include Barroz: Nidhi Kaakkum Bootham, Bienvenidos al Lolita, A Private Affair, Manuela, etc.

The beautiful dancer began her training by studying Spanish Dance and Dramatic Art at the Superior Conservatory of Dance in Seville.

She continued her career in various productions such as Amor Brujo, Carmen, Don Juan en los Ruedos, etc.

She landed a leading role by starring in the television series Welcome to Lolita. In the show, she portrayed Roxy, a trans character with a complex and risky personality.
