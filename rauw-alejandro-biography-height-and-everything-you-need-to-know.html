Rauw Alejandro Biography, Height And Everything You Need To Know

 Posted on August 27, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  704 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Puerto Rican rapper, singer, and songwriter by the name of Rauw Alejandro was born in San Juan, Puerto Rico, on January 10, 1993. The name Ral Alejandro Ocasio Ruiz is his birth name.

Rauw developed a passion for music as a child and began penning songs at a young age. Several musical genres, including reggaeton, R&B, and hip-hop, had an impact on him.

Rauw Alejandro began posting his songs to SoundCloud in 2014, where he developed a little fan base. The next year, in 2018, he signed with the record company Duars Entertainment and dropped his debut single, “Tattoo.” On YouTube, the song amassed millions of views after becoming popular.

The fusion of several genres of Rauw’s music and his distinctive singing style have made him well known. His most well-known tracks include “Fantasas,” “Tattoo Remix,” “La Tóxica,” and other tunes with Camilo.

Rauw Alejandro Height

Rauw Alejandro is 5 feet 10 inches (178 cm) tall, which is his height. He has black hair and brown eyes.

Rauw is well known for his sense of style in addition to music, and he has been in numerous fashion ads. Additionally, he has worked with a number of well-known companies, including Adidas and Puma.

Rauw Alejandro Career

His second studio album, “Vice Versa,” containing the lead single “Todo de Ti,” was released on June 25, 2021. His debut studio album, “Afrodisaco,” was published on November 13, 2020.

The album “Afrodisaco” reached at number 2 on the Productores de Msica de Espaa chart, which is the national body in charge of Spain’s music charts, and number 3 on the US Latin music chart. It had received ‘Gold’ certification from both the Productores de Msica de Espaa and the RIAA (Recording Industry Association of America).

“Vice Versa” reached its highest point at the top of both the US Latin and the Productores de Msica de Espaa charts. The RIAA had awarded it ‘Platinum’ rating. He made his recording debut with the release of Punto de “Equilibrio” in November 2016. He is a member of “the new generation” of urban vocalists from Puerto Rico.

Rauw Alejandro Net Worth

As of 2021, Rauw Alejandro’s net worth is anticipated to be approximately $15 million. His primary source of money is the music business.

Even though his exact yearly pay and career profits have not been disclosed, there is no question in the minds of his supporters that he is earning a sizable sum of money from his work.

Alejandro Rauw’s spouse

Alejandro Rauw has not wed yet and plans to do so in the future. His beautiful girlfriend Rosala, whose real name is Rosala Vila Tobella, is the person he is dating right now. Rosala is a popular contemporary flamenco performer, lyricist, producer, and composer from Spain.

The couple first started dating in 2021, and they are currently living happily together with no problems. When they were spotted holding hands in Los Angeles in August, the duo first fueled dating rumors.

Over the weekend, Rauw and Rosalia posted social media updates showcasing one another, starting with a TikTok published by Rosala, 28, on Friday. The “Lo Vas A Olvidar” singer spun around her boyfriend, Alejandro, 28, as they formed a heart shape with their arms in the video.

Rosalia celebrated her birthday on Saturday and commemorated the occasion by posting a number of pictures to Instagram. She can be seen in one image lying on Alejandro’s lap while he plays video games. He has never been married and has never had kids.
