Luis Enrique Wife – Elena Cullel is the wife of the Spanish football legend, Luis Enrique. This article tells you all you need to know about her

But before looking at her life we should first take a look at who Luis Enrique is.

Luis Enrique Martínez García, popularly known as Luis Enrique with the nickname, Lucho, is a retired footballer and currently operates as a professional football Manager.
Luis Enrique is currently the Spanish National Team’s Head Coach.

He started his professional football career in 1989-1990 when he joined Sporting Gijón B. He later got promoted to the senior side, Sporting Gijón, in the very same year where he made 45 appearances and scored 17 goals for the La Liga side before joining the Spanish giants, Real Madrid.

Luis Enrique joined Real Madrid in 1991 and spent 5 years with the Kings of Madrid making 213 appearances and registering 18 goals to his name.

He is part of the very few people that made a direct move from Real Madrid to FC Barcelona. Luis Enrique joined FC Barcelona in the 1996-1997 season. He became a Barcelona legend in his 8 years stay and scored 109 goals in his 300 appearances.

Luis Enrique started his professional coaching career in 2008 when he took over Barcelona B. He then moved to AS Roma and later Celta de Vigo before returning to Catalonia to be FC Barcelona Head Coach.
He took over FC Barcelona on 19th May 2014. He spent an amazing 3 years with his former club and exited at the end of his contract on 30th June 2017.

Luis Enrique was announced to be taking over the Spanish National Team on 9th June 2018.

Luis Enrique’s wife: Meet Elena Cullel

Luis Enrique started a relationship with Elena Cullel in 1995 and eventually settled with her by tying the knot with her in 1997.

The couple has been together for about 25 years.

Who is Elena Cullel?

Elena Cullel is an Economist who was born in Spain. She is currently 51 years old as she was born in 1971 and only a year younger than her spouse, Luis Enrique.

Elena is well known because of the popularity of her husband yet she tries to live a low-profile life away from her husband’s fame.
