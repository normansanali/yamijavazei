AEW Adds Christian Cage Segment To 12/23 Collision Card

 Posted on September 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  380 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

AEW has a loaded episode of Collision geared up for the December 22nd episode. That Holiday Bash edition of the show got a bit bigger as well.

Ringside News provided comprehensive coverage of AEW Dynamite this week. As with any live show, you never know what can happen to throw wrench into plans.

Thunder Rosa is set to return to the ring for the first time since October 2022. During AEW Collision this week, they announced that the former AEW Women’s World Champion will wrestle in a tag team match.

AEW Collision is going through San Antonio next week, Thunder Rosa’s home crowd, and they will get to welcome her back to the ring. They announced that Thunder Rosa will team with Abadon to take on Julia Hart and Skye Blue.

Scheduled for AEW Collision on December 22nd is an action-packed card that features Skye Blue and Julia Hart facing off against Thunder Rosa and Abadon. The AEW World Trios Championship will be on the line as The Acclaimed & Daddy Ass defend their title against Top Flight & Action Andretti.

In an exciting AEW Continental Classic matchup, Eddie Kingston is set to take on Andrade el Idolo. Additionally, the Continental Classic will showcase two compelling battles: Bryan Danielson vs. Claudio Castagnoli and Brody King vs. Daniel Garcia. Adding to the anticipation, Christian Cage is poised to respond to Adam Copeland’s challenge, promising a night of thrilling encounters on December 22nd at AEW Collision.

Thunder Rosa has been on the Spanish commentary team for AEW for a while now. She was also shown at the beginning of AEW Collision this week, opening up the show.

AEW Collision December 23rd Card:

Skye Blue and Julia Hart vs Thunder Rosa and Abadon

Brian Cage vs Keith Lee

The Acclaimed & Daddy Ass (c) vs. Top Flight & Action Andretti – AEW World Trios Championship

Eddie Kingston vs. Andrade el Idolo – AEW Continental Classic

AEW Continental Classic: Bryan Danielson vs. Claudio Castagnoli – AEW Continental Classic

AEW Continental Classic: Brody King vs. Daniel Garcia – AEW Continental Classic

Christian Cage will answer Adam Copeland’s challenge
