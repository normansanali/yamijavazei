Like many families who have been on reality TV for years, the Roloffs have had some rough times. Fans weren't happy with Audrey and Jeremy's parenting and there were dark allegations against Jacob Roloff. Even when things are going well for the cast members, drama follows, like when fans had a theory about Amy Roloff and Chris Marek's marriage.

When the trailer dropped for season 25 of TLC's Little People, Big World, it was clear that the Roloffs were still dealing with lots of emotions and getting into arguments. Even though the family members have each amassed a high net worth since first becoming famous, they don't have an easy time. In this article, we'll take a look at the relationships between the stars of Little People, Big World, along with how the cast members make money.

Inside The Little People, Big World Cast Members' Dramatic Family Relationships

Little People, Big World season 25 premiered on February 27th, 2024 and viewers learned that Matt Roloff and his kids struggled to get along. While the Roloff family might all have tons of money in the bank, that doesn't mean that they can put aside their differences.

According to People, Zach Roloff and his wife Tori shared that they didn't get together with Matt much. Matt was upset and said:

"My job as a grandpa is to love my grandkids and that's the sad part, you never get all that time back."

How Does The Little People, Big World Cast Make Money?

The Little People, Big World cast earns good salaries starring on the TLC reality series, which explains why they have such high net worths. According to Distractify, it seems likely that Zack Roloff and his wife Tori Roloff were earning $7,000-$10,000 per episode in the last few seasons.

The publication also noted that according to an agent quoted in Business Insider, the payments for reality stars can vary. But stars on a documentary series such as Little People, Big World earn $1,500-$3,000 per episode. If the show gets more seasons, they might get paid $7,000-$1,000 per episode. It seems likely that Matt and Amy Roloff were paid in that salary range.
