Bella Hadid Posts Sweet Couple Pics With Boyfriend Marc Kalman

 Posted on September 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  310 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Bella Hadid is sharing her love with the world.

The supermodel, who is usually keeps her love life quite private, has posted new couple pics of herself and her longtime boyfriend, art director Marc Kalman, on Instagram. In the adorable pics, the pair share a kiss on an NYC sidewalk, as they hold each other and Hadid pops her leg up in the classic pose.

For the snaps, the model wears an updated schoolgirl look, pairing a pleated jean miniskirt with tan jacket featuring black striped sleeves. She completes the outfit with white knee-high socks, black and brown running shoes, and a pair of thin square sunglasses perched atop her bangs.

Kalman's casual fit includes a black sleeveless tank, blue trousers, and black shoes.

Hadid first went public with her relationship in 2021, sharing romantic and PDA-filled photos in multiple social media posts. While most of those pics were included within slideshows, the new post marks the first time a pic with Kalman has been shared on her main grid.

The couple was recently spotted having a blast during their sun-kissed vacation in St. Barts following the model's appearances at the 75th Cannes Film Festival. In photos, the pair exchanged PDA while sunbathing, swimming, and taking pictures of each other.

In a recent interview, Hadid said that she has worked hard to keep her romance with the art director private, in contrast to her previous, heavily-publicized relationships.
